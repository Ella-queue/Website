Home

Welcome to the official website of Toolbox Trainers, the ideal place to develop your scientific skills!

What We Do

Here at Toolbox Trainers, we offer paid, easily accessible internships that allow you to build up your abilities, as well as gain more experience. Having our name on a resume is sure to get you ahead in the game.

Locations

The main building is located on 123 Street St, New York, NY. This is where you will find most of our workforce. We also have many smaller establishments across North America and Europe, see our About Us page for more information.

History

Our founder, Dr. Jett Jackson, established this company as a way to help his peers make it in a competitive field. Dr. Jackson is very passionate about providing guidance to those in need of it, and decided that the best way to do that, was to allow others to work temporarily for his many companies, through a singular program. This is how Toolbox Trainers was originally founded, back in 1995.

Services

Here at Toolbox Trainers, we allow those who are ambitious about the science field to get internships at one of Dr. Jett Jackson's many companies. This gives the employees valuable experience for their resumes, as many of our founder's companies are regarded as quite prestigious.
