About Us

Our goal is simple--we want to make it easier for people interested in the sciences to make it big in such a competitive field.

Our Story

We were founded in September of 1995 by Dr. Jett Jackson, who saw many of his fellow scientists struggling to find work. He decided that he has seen enough, and started Toolbox Trainers as a way to inspire and help people to get the careers they want.
